Our Story

When we talk about Indian food, the common items that everyone think of is Roti, Naan, Butter chicken etc.., but Indian cuisine is much more than that. The southern indian cuisine is unpopular among most of the people. We decided to open this Restaurant so that everyone can experience the unexplored foods from the south Indian Cuisine.
